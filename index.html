Episode.Next

A Web App to Track the Watching of Your Favorite Shows

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

Episode.Next Overview

Keep track of your favorite shows to know what you need to watch! It's deployed on Google App Engine here: https://thewirewatcher.appspot.com

Add all of the series your watching into Episode.Next and it will show the next unplayed episode of each show. Once you've watched it just clicked "Played" or clicked the "Play and Share" icon to share the fact that you've watched to Facebook.

Episode.Next Sync

Link Episode.Next to your dropbox account or your Google account and it will sync your latest episodes across your devices. Using Google is substantially faster in the syncing department than Dropbox.
